Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email to create a new password.
How do I transfer money to another account?
Navigate to the "Transfers" section in your dashboard. Select the account to transfer from, enter the recipient's details and amount, then confirm the transaction. Transfers are typically processed within 1-2 business days.
What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?
Immediately call our 24/7 support line at 1-800-APEX-BANK to report your card as lost or stolen. We will cancel your current card and issue a replacement, which will arrive within 5-7 business days.
How do I set up automatic bill payments?
Go to the "Bill Pay" section in your account, select "Set Up Automatic Payments", choose the payee and payment frequency, and authorize the recurring payments. You can modify or cancel these at any time.
What are the daily withdrawal limits?
Standard daily ATM withdrawal limit is $500, and point-of-sale purchase limit is $3,000. These limits can be adjusted temporarily by contacting customer service or through your online account settings.
How do I update my personal information?
You can update your contact information in the "Profile" section of your online account. For changes to your legal name or address, please visit a branch location with appropriate documentation.

Security Tips

  • Never share your password, PIN, or security questions with anyone
  • Enable two-factor authentication for added security
  • Regularly monitor your account activity and report suspicious transactions immediately
  • Use strong, unique passwords for your banking accounts
  • Be cautious of phishing emails pretending to be from your bank
  • Always log out of your banking session when finished
